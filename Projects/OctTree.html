Visualization of Frustum Culling using Octree


Programmer : Myself


Click HERE to open the demo in new tab. [Note : Google announced of stopping the hosting WebGL and other services, the demo might stop working soon.]

Controls:
W, A, S, D for moving around.
Space for toggling between the control of main camera and frustum (trapezoid).
Right click to toggle between following modes:
  • Default: Spawn one particle in front of the camera.
  • Multiple: Spawn multiple uniformly distributed particles.
For getting out of the application keep the Escape Key Pressed.

No questions about the extra particle in the system.
