Visualization of Frustum Culling using Quadtree


Programmer : Myself


Click HEREto open the demo in new tab. [Note : Google announced of stopping the hosting WebGL and other services, the demo might stop working soon.]

Controls:
W, A, S, D : For the moving of the frustum around the scene.
Right click : To toggle the spawning of particles to dragging particles.
At any time you can move the points of the frustum to increase or decrease it. JUST MAKE SURE, THE CENTER ORANGE-COLORED POINT NEEDS TO BE INSIDE THE FRUSTUM.
